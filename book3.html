* Project Overview *

I developed this data physicalization system in an experience design fabrication course at Northeastern University.

The object enables the collection of personal, emotional data as well as the storage of all neccesary pieces to make the visualization. The ultimate goal is to assist the user with self reflection about their emotions, as well as to allow for the sharing of the often complex emotions with those close to them, even non-verbally. The target audience for such an object are people who struggle to understand their own emotions, as well as to communicate them to others. It could be thought of as a visual journal.
